Quick URL's

These URLs will allow you to quickly check your evio stats, player nfts, nft information, and more!
https://evinfo.vercel.app?username={username} Usage: Fetches User status
https://evinfo.vercel.app?nft_id={nft_token} Usage: Searches up Nft's info
https://evinfo.vercel.app?equipped_skins={username} Usage: Fetches users current equipped skins
https://evinfo.vercel.app?e_to_usdc={e} Usage: Shows how much e will equal to usdc (this wont last long)
https://evinfo.vercel.app?nft_lookup={username} Usage: Fetches all the players Nft's/none Nft's they own
